About us

We are a fast-food restaurant chain that specialized in fried chicken, operating across the southwestern United States. The name, pronounced [los ˈpo.ʝos eɾˈma.nos], is ungrammatical Spanish for "The Chicken Brothers." Our Restaurant was founded by Gustavo Fring and Max Arciniega, the restaurant chain has fourteen locations throughout the southwest and was Gustavo Fring's major business concern.

Our most famous cooks are Heisenberg (alias Walter White) and The Kapn(alias Jessie Pinkman).
